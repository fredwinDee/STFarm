Stone Terrace Farm

**** Site under construction. ****

We are a small family homestead of about 25 acres located in northern central Maryland.

About 16 acres of the property is wooded and under a Forest Management Plan, another 2 acres approved for a pollinator meadow, and the remaining acreage is occupied by an heirloom apple orchard, and various other fruit crop plantings, fields and gardens.

Most of our electricity is generated by a grid-tied, ground mounted solar array.
